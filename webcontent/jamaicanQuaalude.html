Cocktail Description

I'll assume that this delicious cocktail's name is derived from its tropical flavors (Jamaican), and its mind numbing effects (Quaalude). With five spirits, and a bit of cream to blend it all together, this rich drink is a great dessert cocktail that will definitely keep the evening going. We hope you'll try our featured cocktail, the Jamaican Quaalude!

Cocktail Details

1 oz   Kahlua
1 oz   Malibu Coconut Rum
1 oz   Baileys Irish Cream
.5 oz   Amaretto
.5   Frangelico
1 oz   Cream

How to make

Combine all of the ingredients in an ice filled cocktail shaker. Cover, shake well, and pour into a Rocks glass. Add a couple of sipping straws, garnish accordingly

Garnish

Slice of Starfruit and/or Cherry to garnish
